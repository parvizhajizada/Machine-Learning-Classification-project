Breast Cancer Classification

Parviz Hajizada

Introduction

Breast Cancer is one of most earliest cancer recorded in human history and have taken countless lives. Even though no reliable and permanent cure have not been developed, detection at early stages is crucial because the sooner the better.

In this paper, we build classification models to identify if the tumor is benign (harmless) or malignant (harmful). This dataset is collected and provided by University of Wisconsin (1995) link. It is composed of 30 descriptive features that are from a digitized image of a fine needle aspirate (FNA) of a breast mass.
